FAQ

How to avoid buying a 'pig in a poke' from Europe?

With Autotrade Group OÜ, it’s easy. We only buy clean cars with no accidents, no repaints, and verified mileage. Our experts have bought over 1500 cars from European auctions and can assess condition from photos and documents. We also check service history with specialized tools for full transparency. With us, you get a great deal and a reliable ride.

What types of damage do we avoid?

We avoid any cars with a history of accidents, repaints, or major defects. We don’t buy vehicles with frame, engine, transmission, or electrical damage, or those with questionable mileage history. Our cars are only clean examples with transparent history.

How to pay for a car and delivery from Europe?

We offer convenient payment methods via our EUR, USD, or GBP accounts. You can transfer funds internationally using our bank details. Auction fees vary, but we help avoid extra costs. Delivery is paid separately via transfer, covering transport to your country.

What guarantees do you provide?

We guarantee a turnkey price without repairs and the car’s integrity during delivery. We also verify mileage and history before purchase. You can also insure the car—any damage en route gets fully covered for repairs.

Which European auctions do we buy cars from?

We work with the best European auctions offering clean cars with no accidents and verified mileage:
Mobile.de — wide selection of clean cars from dealers with transparent history.
Autorola — leased cars in good condition with verified mileage.
BCA (British Car Auctions) — one of Europe’s largest auctions, where we pick clean leased cars.
Exleasingcar — specializes in leased cars with guaranteed true mileage.
UsedAutoBank — a platform with verified cars and transparent history.
